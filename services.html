Health Care

Control of infection. Operational integrity. Healthcare experienced operatives.

All of the above are key to working in healthcare environments, thinking about the use of the building or area, how it affects the 'working day' and the impact it has on the staff and end user.

These highly sensitive and challenging areas requite a thought process and installation process that is individual - from the change of use of a room to the re-refurbishment of a ward - every detail and impact has to be thought out and planned in advance - meaning that our understanding of these areas becomes and remains paramount.

Education

Providing an education establishment with 'fit for purpose' flooring can be challenging, given that areas tend to receive heavy footfall, have a need to have a safety element and often require a contemporary feel.

Our design and specification skills come under scrutiny from everyone including senior heads of departments, the technicians that have to clean and maintain the floors and the students that utilise the facilities.

Our consultants & surveying team can be called in to advise on specific areas, provide technical advice to help the life cycle of the flooring & deliver projects encompassing the building's needs & the end user's vision.

As an established flooring provider for the education sector, Contraflor can respond to small repairs/maintenance programmes, student accommodation, technical learning environments, offices and associated areas.

Providing procurement and maintenance teams with cost effective, design led flooring solutions to suit any budget.
