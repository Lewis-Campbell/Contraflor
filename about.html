Why choose Contraflor

Our long term relationships also extend to our suppliers. Contraflor deal directly with flooring manufacturers based both in the UK and Europe. This enables us to buy competitively and pass on additional savings.

Our aim for all our clients is to "get it right, first time, every time"

Committed to the Environment

Contraflor is proud to hold its ISO 14001 certificate, providing the commitment that the company has in considering the enviornment. In a world where sustainability and corporate responsibility are ever more prevalent, adoption of the criteria contained in ISO 14001 is a tried and tested approach to demonstrate a company's commitment in reducing its environmental impact.

Contraflor recycles 100% of carboard and paper waste and over 85% of plastic waste (by weight). In addition, our policy of pre-cutting materials before they leave our warehouse ensures less revisits to sites and enables full re-cycling of 100% of waste materials. Utilising a national waste carrier to collect from our clients sites and further sort out our waste for recyclable items as part of our work processes further reduces our impact on the environment.

We also recognise the need to reduce, wherever possible, our carbon footprint and therefore recruit strategically placed fitting teams int he local geographical area of major contracts wherever viable. CO2 emissions are further reduced by our in house contract team mapping out weekly deliveries for best value fuel consumption. Using a specialist contract team at head office enables us to keep emissions low by mapping out designated routes for all our fitting teams.
