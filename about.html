What we Offer

UNIQUE in its approach to flooring, Contraflor aim to be the only choice for private and public sector organisations. With a designated team specialising in education we are pleased to offer a flooring service that incorporates all aspects of flooring in one company - making Contraflor a company that can deliver.

With our DIVERSE team of consultants, we are capable of providing a service unrivalled by any other flooring contractor. Each consultant has their own specialist discipline and this ensures that we 'get it right, first time, every time'.

To supply and fit floors to the public sector is challenging and requires a SPECIALISED contractor to ensure all the boxes are ticked and everything is to a consistently high standard. Contraflor establish relationships based on their knowledge, expertise and fundamental understanding of their clients - making us a specialist to every client we work with.

As well as every client receiving advice from a dedicated consultant surveyor, our customer service department and after sales service provide an unrivalled service in our sector.

Committed to the Environment

Contraflor is proud to hold its ISO 14001 certificate, providing the commitment that the company has in considering the enviornment. In a world where sustainability and corporate responsibility are ever more prevalent, adoption of the criteria contained in ISO 14001 is a tried and tested approach to demonstrate a company's commitment in reducing its environmental impact.

Contraflor recycles 100% of carboard and paper waste and over 85% of plastic waste (by weight). In addition, our policy of pre-cutting materials before they leave our warehouse ensures less revisits to sites and enables full re-cycling of 100% of waste materials. Utilising a national waste carrier to collect from our clients sites and further sort out our waste for recyclable items as part of our work processes further reduces our impact on the environment.

We also recognise the need to reduce, wherever possible, our carbon footprint and therefore recruit strategically placed fitting teams int he local geographical area of major contracts wherever viable. CO2 emissions are further reduced by our in house contract team mapping out weekly deliveries for best value fuel consumption. Using a specialist contract team at head office enables us to keep emissions low by mapping out designated routes for all our fitting teams.

The Quotation Process

The project manager that visited your building will have taken measurements of all the areas that you require flooring works for. In certain circumstances they may also have taken measurements of other areas for us to use for future requirements.

They will also have undertaken an inspection of all your subfloors to determine what, if any, floor preparation may be required following uplift of your existing flooring. Full inspections are difficult to undertake as existing flooring is normally fully adhered to your subfloor.

You should receive your quotation within five days of our project manager's visit.

Delivery Times

We hold extensive stocks in our 12,000 square feet of warehousing and can normally supply standard range carpets and vinyl for small to medium sized jobs within ten days.

Larger installations can normally be achieved within four weeks from the date of order.

If you are having other works undertaken before carpets are required, please let your project manager know in advance and we shall programme works accoridngly.
